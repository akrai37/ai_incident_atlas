About this view

  • Sample of 480 incidents stratified across 5 sectors (transportation, media/content, public safety, healthcare, finance) drawn from the AI Incident Database.
  • Pathway stages extracted by Claude (Sonnet 4.6) from incident reports, with blind self-validation on a 30-incident subset (see writeup).
  • The missed intervention stage is annotated only when the source text documents a specific prior warning or skipped check. For roughly 47% of incidents it is null, meaning the report itself does not describe a precursor that anyone saw and ignored. That null rate is a finding, not a gap.
  • AIID is biased toward English-language, US/EU, consumer-facing incidents; this view inherits that bias.
